ajglobe — examples

fast orthographic globes, straight from the source — no build step

  • cells-to-poly

    the globes from the cells_to_poly blog post, rendered from GeoJSON — concave polygons with holes, winding-aware fills, per-feature styling, synced rotation.

  • reference-detail

    coastlines, country borders, cities, graticule, small-circle rings — Natural Earth fetched from a CDN at three detail levels.

  • dggs-compare

    two rotationally-synced globes: H3 res 1 vs res 2, cells colored by skar aspect ratio; hover for cell id + AR.

  • mu3-ar

    mu3 (a prototype icosahedral aperture-7 DGGS) colored by aspect ratio, switchable resolution. res 0–4 here; 5–6 need the local torture-test binaries.

  • dggs-globe

    every ivea7h cell with per-cell color, opacity fade, PNG export, keyboard rotation. r1–r3 here; the r5/r6 torture tests (168k / 1.18M cells) need the local binaries — see the README.
